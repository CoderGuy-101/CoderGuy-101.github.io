Quick Tips

🚗 Transport

  • Pre-book taxi from Dabolim
  • Negotiate full-day rates
  • AC cars are worth it in monsoon humidity

🌧️ Monsoon Essentials

  • Raincoats over umbrellas for trekking
  • Waterproof bags for phones/cameras
  • Extra clothes — you will get wet
  • Dry footwear for indoors

🍛 Must Eat

  • Fish curry rice — Goan staple
  • Prawn masala at beach shacks
  • Sol kadi (coconut-kokam drink)
  • Bebinca — Goan layered dessert

⚡ Book Ahead

  • Dudhsagar jeep/trek permit
  • Mystic Woods timing slots
  • Science Centre show timings
  • Mall de Goa bowling on weekends
